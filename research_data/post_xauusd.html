Chapter 1: SMC Core Glossary & Structure Definition

SMC is a language of "equilibrium". Before diving into strategies, we must unify the definitions of basic market vocabulary to distinguish **True Structure** from **Inducement Structure**.

结构破坏 (BOS)Break of Structure (BOS)
定义： 价格实体在 **主要/次要趋势结构** 中，以 **收盘价** 明确突破了上一个 **Protected High/Low**。这标志着 **Interbank Price Delivery Algorithm (IPDA)** 持续向特定方向交付价格的意图得到 **Confirmed Continuation**。
Definition: Price candle body closes above/below a previous **Protected High/Low** in the primary/secondary trend. This signifies **Confirmed Continuation** of the **Interbank Price Delivery Algorithm's (IPDA)** intent to continue delivering price in that direction.
结构转变 (CHoCH)Change of Character (CHoCH)
定义： 价格反向打破了导致最新 BOS 的那个 **次级支撑/阻力** (通常是最近的 **Inducement Low/High**)。它预示着 **Higher Time Frame (HTF) Bias** 发生潜在转换，是 **Mitigation & Reversal** 阶段的 **早期确认**。
Definition: Price breaks the "last higher low/lower high" that caused the latest BOS. It signals a potential change in **Higher Time Frame (HTF) Bias** and is the **earliest confirmation** of the **Mitigation & Reversal** phase.
流动性扫荡 (Liquidity Sweep)Liquidity Sweep / Raid
定义： 价格的 **Wick (影线)** 穿透了上一个有效高点/低点，但 **K线实体 (Body)** 未能收盘突破。这是一种 **Manipulation** 行为，目的是收集止损订单并为机构提供 **Optimal Entry** 的对冲流动性。
Definition: The price **Wick** penetrates a previous valid high/low, but the **Candle Body** fails to close beyond it. This **Manipulation** gathers stop-loss orders to provide **Optimal Entry** hedging liquidity for institutions.

Chapter 2: Order Blocks (OB) & Repricing Mechanisms

OBs are the footprints of institutional accumulation. Institutional process: **Manipulate → Accumulate → Expand → Retest/Mitigate**.

订单块 (Order Block)Order Block (OB) / POI
定义： 导致 **Significant Displacement (剧烈扩张)** 或 **BOS** 之前的 **最后一根反向 K 线**。它代表了机构在市场中留下的 **Unmitigated Orders (未平抑订单)** 集中区。其有效性取决于其是否 **Swept Liquidity**。
Definition: The **last opposing candle** before a **Break of Structure (BOS)** or violent **Displacement**. It represents an area of **Unmitigated Orders** left by institutions. Its validity is amplified if it **Swept Liquidity**.
平抑块 (Mitigation Block)Mitigation Block
定义： 机构在首次推动价格后未能完全平抑的 **Remaining Order (剩余订单)** 所在的区域。价格返回此区域时，常引发第二次反向平抑，即 **Re-entry for the institution**。
Definition: The area where the **Remaining Orders** that institutions failed to fully mitigate after the initial price push reside. A return to this zone often triggers a second, balancing move; a **Re-entry for the institution**.

第 3 章：价格失衡与 FVG 机制

FVG 是市场效率低下的表现。由于订单挤压和不平衡撮合，IPDA 总会试图修复它。这是价格的 **吸力中心 (Center of Gravity)**。

Chapter 3: Imbalance (IMB) & Fair Value Gap (FVG)

FVGs represent market inefficiency. Due to order compression and matching imbalance, the IPDA will always attempt to reprice them. They act as the price's **Center of Gravity**.

公允价值缺口 (FVG)Fair Value Gap (FVG)
定义： 三根连续K线中，**C1 的影线** 与 **C3 的影线** 之间未能发生价格交互的 **Inefficient Pricing Range**。IPDA 的核心任务之一是 **Reprice (重新定价)** 回到 **FVG EQ (中点)** 进行平衡。
Definition: The **Inefficient Pricing Range** where the wick of the 1st candle and the wick of the 3rd candle in a 3-candle sequence fail to overlap. A core IPDA task is to **Reprice** back to the **FVG EQ (midpoint)** for balancing.
平衡价格范围 (BPR)Balanced Price Range (BPR)
定义： 两个重叠的、但方向相反的 FVG。此区域通常被视为市场已经达到 **Efficiency (效率)**，因此不是理想的入场兴趣点，但可作为 **Potential Target**。
Definition: Two overlapping FVGs of opposite directions. This area is generally considered **Efficient**, hence not an ideal entry point, but it serves as a **Potential Target**.

IPDA 使用 **Higher Time Frame (HTF) Bias** 作为导航，将价格推向下一个 **Primary Liquidity Target (主要流动性目标)**。

The market cycles through four stages: **Accumulation → Expansion → Retracement/Mitigation → Reversal**. Every **Expansion** is a commitment to mitigate pending orders and reprice imbalances (FVG).

The IPDA uses **Higher Time Frame (HTF) Bias** for navigation, driving price toward the next **Primary Liquidity Target**.

第 7 章：多时间框架分析 (Top-Down Analysis)

交易成功在于对市场 **Hiearchy (层次结构)** 的理解。HTF (高时间框架) 决定方向，LTF (低时间框架) 决定时机。

Chapter 7: Multi-Timeframe (Top-Down) Analysis

Trading success lies in understanding market **Hierarchy**. **HTF determines the Bias; LTF determines the Timing.**

  • 宏观 (Macro / HTF)： 月线/周线/日线 —— 识别 **Primary Structure** 和 **Major Liquidity Pool**，决定交易的 **Bias (偏向)**。
  • 中观 (Intermediate / MTF)： 4小时/1小时 —— 识别 **Key Point of Interest (POI)**：如未平抑的 **Daily OB** 或 **FVG**，这是我们计划入场的区域。
  • 微观 (Micro / LTF)： 15分钟/5分钟/1分钟 —— 观察价格到达 POI 后的 **Confirmation (确认)** 行为，例如 **CHoCH** 或 **Sweep & Rejection**，决定入场触发 (Trigger)。
  • Macro (HTF): Monthly/Weekly/Daily — Identifies **Primary Structure** and **Major Liquidity Pools**, determines trading **Bias**.
  • Intermediate (MTF): H4/H1 — Identifies **Key Points of Interest (POI)**: such as unmitigated **Daily OBs** or **FVGs**. This is the zone for planning entries.
  • Micro (LTF): M15/M5/M1 — Observes **Confirmation** when price arrives at the POI, e.g., **CHoCH** or **Sweep & Rejection**, determining the Entry Trigger.

Chapter 8: Strong vs. Weak Structure: Target & Protection

Distinguishing between **Strong High/Low** and **Weak High/Low** is the cornerstone of risk management and target setting. Protect the Strong, Target the Weak.

强高点/低点 (Strong High/Low)Strong High/Low
导致了结构破坏 (BOS) 并创出新低/高点的反向高点/低点。它是 **受保护的 (Protected)**，机构会捍卫这个位置，因为它是 **Order Accumulation** 的起点。这是放置 **Stop Loss** 的最佳点。
A high/low that caused a BOS and created a new extreme. It is **Protected**; institutions will defend it as it marks the beginning of **Order Accumulation**. The ideal place for a **Stop Loss**.

未能推动价格创新低/高的反向高点/低点。它是 **目标 (Target)**，价格大概率会突破这里去扫损（形成 **Equal Highs/Lows**）。永远不要在弱高点上方做空，因为那是 **Buy-Side Liquidity (BSL)** 的聚集区。
A high/low that failed to break structure. It is a **Target**; price will likely run through it to sweep liquidity (forming **Equal Highs/Lows**). Never short above a weak high, as it is a **Buy-Side Liquidity (BSL)** pool.

第 9 章：高阶结构模型与三协补单结构

在理解了基础的市场结构（BOS/CHoCH）之后，我们需要解决一个实战中最大的痛点：“既然结构已经破坏，为什么回踩之后不延续趋势，反而继续深跌？”

这是因为市场并非线性的“突破-回踩-延续”，而是一个三维的流动性平衡过程。L.K. Capital 将这一过程定义为“三协补单结构” (Three-Phase Retracement)。

核心定义：三协补单结构 (Three-Phase Retracement) 三协结构 (Three-Phase Structure)： 指在主要趋势发起前或延续中，价格必然经历的三个特定阶段：
1. 扫荡 (Sweep)： 价格以影线形式清除 **Inducement Liquidity** (内部流动性，如 Equal Lows/Highs)。
2. 回归/平抑 (Return / Mitigation)： 价格精确回踩 **Premium/Discount Zone** 内的 **Unmitigated OB** 或 **Breaker**，填补 **FVG**。
3. 扩张 (Expansion)： 在 LTF 确认 **CHoCH** (Trigger) 后，价格发生剧烈推动，释放真实的趋势。

Chapter 9: Advanced Structure & Three-Phase Retracement

After understanding basic Market Structure (BOS/CHoCH), we must address the biggest pain point in live trading: "Why does price break structure, retrace, and then fail to continue?"

This happens because the market is a 3-dimensional process of liquidity balancing. At L.K. Capital, we define this as the **"Three-Phase Retracement"** structure.

Core Definition: Three-Phase Retracement Three-Phase Structure: A mandatory sequence price undergoes before or during a major trend:
1. Sweep: Price wicks through **Inducement Liquidity** (internal LQs, e.g., Equal Lows/Highs).
2. Return (Mitigation): Price precisely returns to an **Unmitigated OB** or **Breaker** within the **Premium/Discount Zone**, filling the **FVG**.
3. Expansion: After an LTF **CHoCH** (Trigger), price violently pushes, releasing the true trend.

第 11 章：诱导结构 (Inducement - IDM)

Chapter 11: Inducement (IDM)

IDM is a trap. Minor support/resistance formed right before the **Real POI** to induce early retail entry. Its purpose is to create **Internal Liquidity** for institutional use.

实战应用： 只有价格清除了 **IDM** 之后，剩余的 **OB/FVG** 才被视为高胜率的 **True POI**。在 IDM 被扫荡之前进场，几乎一定会遭遇止损。

Practical Application: Only after price has cleared the **IDM** should the remaining **OB/FVG** be considered a high-probability **True POI**. Entering before the IDM sweep is almost guaranteed to result in a stop-out.

Chapter 12: Advanced Order Block (OB) Filters

第 13 章：高胜率入场模型详解 (Detailed Entry Models)

我们将入场策略分为三类。成功的关键在于等待 **Confluence (共振)**。以下模型假设你已经确定了 **HTF POI**。

Chapter 13: High-Probability Entry Models Detailed

We classify entry strategies into three types. The key to success is waiting for **Confluence**. The following models assume you have already identified an **HTF POI**.

Model A: 极端位置延续 (Extreme Continuation)

这是最安全的顺势模型。当价格回踩到趋势的 **Extreme Discount/Premium** 区域时使用。

  • Entry: 在 CHoCH 发生后，价格回踩新形成的 **M15 FVG** 进场。
  • Stop: 放置在刚形成的 **Swing Low** 下方 2-3 pips。

Model B: 海龟汤 / 流动性猎杀 (Turtle Soup / Liquidity Raid)

这是反转模型，用于捕捉高点/低点的假突破。

  • Setup: 价格接近显著的 **Equal Highs (EQH)** 或 **Daily High**。
  • Action: 价格以 **Wick (影线)** 刺穿高点，但实体收盘在下方 (SFP - Swing Failure Pattern)。
  • Confirmation: 下一根 K 线必须是看跌吞没 (Engulfing) 或伴随巨大的卖出量。
  • Entry: 激进者在 SFP 收盘时进场；保守者等待 LTF CHoCH。

Model C: 失败反转结构 (Breaker Reversal)

当预期的 OB 失守时，利用它作为反向支撑/阻力。

  • Context: 价格强力击穿了看涨 OB，没有反应。
  • Shift: 此 OB 现在变成了 **Breaker Block**。
  • Entry: 等待价格回抽测试 Breaker Block 的底部 (Bearish) 进场做空。

Model A: Extreme Continuation

The safest trend-following model. Used when price retraces to the **Extreme Discount/Premium** zone of the trend.

  • Setup: HTF Trend is Bullish. Price retraces to an H4 Order Block.
  • Trigger: On M15, price **Sweeps** Asian Lows or Previous Candle Low, then immediately reclaims, forming a **CHoCH**.
  • Entry: Enter on the retracement to the newly formed **M15 FVG** after the CHoCH.
  • Stop: Placed 2-3 pips below the newly formed **Swing Low**.

Model B: Turtle Soup / Liquidity Raid

A reversal model used to catch fake-outs at major highs/lows.

  • Setup: Price approaches significant **Equal Highs (EQH)** or **Daily High**.
  • Action: Price pierces the high with a **Wick**, but the Body closes below (SFP - Swing Failure Pattern).
  • Confirmation: The next candle must be Bearish Engulfing or accompanied by high sell volume.
  • Entry: Aggressive: Enter on SFP close; Conservative: Wait for LTF CHoCH.

Model C: Breaker Reversal

Using a failed OB as support/resistance.

  • Context: Price violently smashes through a Bullish OB with no reaction.
  • Shift: This OB is now a **Breaker Block**.
  • Entry: Wait for price to retest the underside of the Breaker Block (Bearish) to go short.

  • Bullish SMT (看涨背离)： 当美元指数 (DXY) 创出更高的高点 (Higher High)，而黄金 (XAUUSD) 拒绝创出更低的低点 (Higher Low)。这意味着黄金具有内在强势，机构正在买入护盘。
  • Bearish SMT (看跌背离)： 当 DXY 创出更低的低点，而黄金 未能创出更高的高点 (Lower High)。

XAUUSD vs XAGUSD (正相关)：

如果黄金创出新高，而白银未能创出新高，这表明多头动能减弱，是一个潜在的做空信号。

DXY vs XAUUSD (Negative Correlation):

  • Bullish SMT: When DXY makes a **Higher High**, but Gold **refuses to make a Lower Low (Higher Low)**. This indicates intrinsic strength in Gold; institutions are accumulating.
  • Bearish SMT: When DXY makes a **Lower Low**, but Gold **fails to make a Higher High (Lower High)**.

XAUUSD vs XAGUSD (Positive Correlation):

If Gold makes a New High, but Silver fails to confirm (Lower High), it suggests weakening bullish momentum and is a potential short signal.

第 16 章：波动率与 ADR 量化边界

不要在价格已经耗尽动能时追单。利用 ADR (Average Daily Range) 确定价格的物理极限。

Chapter 16: Volatility & ADR Quantitative Boundaries

Do not chase price when momentum is exhausted. Use ADR (Average Daily Range) to determine the physical limits of price.

ADR 计算： 过去 5 天或 10 天的最高价与最低价之差的平均值。对于黄金，正常的 ADR 约为 $25 - $35 (视波动率周期而定)。

实战应用规则：

  1. 如果在伦敦开盘前，价格已经走了 80% 的 ADR，当天大概率是震荡市。
  2. 如果价格在纽约时段触及 **HTF POI**，同时该位置正好是 **当日低点 + 1.5倍 ADR** 的距离，这是极高胜率的反转做空点。因为机构极少在一天内推动超过 2 倍 ADR 的行情。

ADR Calculation: The average of the High-Low range over the last 5 or 10 days. For Gold, a typical ADR is ~$25 - $35 (depending on volatility cycle).

Practical Rules:

  1. If price has already covered 80% of its ADR before London Open, the day is likely to be choppy/ranging.
  2. If price hits an **HTF POI** during NY Session, and this level aligns with **Daily Low + 1.5x ADR**, it is an extremely high-probability **Reversal Short**. Institutions rarely push price beyond 2x ADR in a single day.

第 17 章：最大痛点路径 (Max Pain & Sentiment)

市场是情绪的反向指标。做市商的利润来自于大多数人的亏损。

Chapter 17: Max Pain Path & Sentiment Analysis

The market is a contrarian indicator of sentiment. Market Maker profit comes from the majority's losses.

做市商库存模型 (Inventory Model)：

做市商是被动接单的。如果散户疯狂买入 (Buy Orders)，做市商必须卖出给他们，从而持有 **净空头 (Net Short)** 库存。为了盈利，做市商必须将价格打压下去。

如何判断？ 观察社交媒体情绪和零售持仓数据。当所有人都在喊“黄金要冲 3000”时，往往是阶段性顶部。IPDA 会寻找下方最近的 **Sell-Side Liquidity (SSL)** 进行清算。

Market Maker Inventory Model:

Market Makers are passive liquidity providers. If retail buys aggressively, MMs must sell to them, resulting in a **Net Short** inventory. To profit, MMs must drive the price down.

How to judge? Observe social sentiment and retail positioning. When everyone screams "Gold to 3000", it is often a local top. The IPDA will target the nearest **Sell-Side Liquidity (SSL)** below.

第 18 章：概率思维与数学期望

交易不是关于“下一次是对是错”，而是关于“下一组 100 次交易的结果”。

Chapter 18: Probabilistic Thinking & Mathematical Expectancy

Trading is not about "being right next time", it's about "the result of the next 100 trades".

期望值公式 (Expectancy)： $$E = (PW \times AW) - (PL \times AL)$$

其中：PW=胜率, AW=平均盈利, PL=败率, AL=平均亏损。

黄金策略的黄金比例： 由于黄金经常扫损，追求 90% 的胜率是不现实的。SMC 策略通常追求 40%-50% 的胜率，但配合 1:3 或 1:5 的盈亏比。哪怕你输掉一半的次数，你依然能赚大钱。

破产风险 (Risk of Ruin)： 永远不要在一笔交易中冒超过账户 1%-2% 的风险。连续 10 次亏损在数学上是必然会发生的（虽然概率低）。如果每笔冒 10% 的风险，你必死无疑。

Expectancy Formula: $$E = (PW \times AW) - (PL \times AL)$$

Where: PW=Win Rate, AW=Avg Win, PL=Loss Rate, AL=Avg Loss.

The Golden Ratio: Due to Gold's volatility, chasing 90% win rate is unrealistic. SMC strategies typically aim for 40%-50% Win Rate but with 1:3 or 1:5 Risk-to-Reward. Even if you lose half the time, you profit massively.

Risk of Ruin: Never risk more than 1%-2% per trade. A streak of 10 losses is mathematically guaranteed to happen eventually. If you risk 10% per trade, bankruptcy is a certainty.
