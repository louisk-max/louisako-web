L.K. Core Execution Protocol

Our structural approach is defined by discipline and a 5-step checklist for every trade setup.

1

Market Structure

Determine macro bias using HTF (H4/D1) structure via BOS/CHoCH confirmation.
2

Liquidity Hunt

Identify retail stop-loss zones (Liquidity Pools) that will be cleared before institutional entry.
3

Target POI

Pinpoint the Point of Interest (POI) – usually a fresh Order Block or FVG.
4

LTF Entry

On LTF (M5/M15), wait for price to hit the POI, sweep internal liquidity, and confirm with a micro CHoCH.
5

Risk Mandate
